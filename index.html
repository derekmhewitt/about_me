Biography

I was born here in Washington in 1983. My family lived here in Washington state until I was almost 10 years old, then we moved to Colorado for rougly 7 years. My dad worked for Boeing at the time and they needed his expertise there. When we returned we moved into a newer home in Milton that my family still lives in. I ended up going to the same high school I would have went to had we never moved and graduated in 2002.

In 2003 I enlisted in the United States Army as a Machinist, job title 44E.

After getting back from my last deployment in 2009 I was ready for a change. I exited active duty and enlisted in the Reserves. I moved back home to Washington and prepared to go back to school. I applied to and was accepted by Central Washington Univerty. I attended CWU for roughly 3 years but never graduated, I switched majors several times and didn't really have a solid plan for what I wanted to accomplish there.

Since exiting college I've worked several odd jobs
